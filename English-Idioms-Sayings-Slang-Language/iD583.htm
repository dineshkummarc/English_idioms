Idioms: swell -- swift


 
IdiomMeaningExample
swell great, wonderful, (often used sarcastically) "How was your vacation?" "Oh swell. It rained every day."
swelled head feeling too proud, let it go to your head If I win the talent contest, I promise not to get a swelled head.
swift intelligent, smart, bright I'm not very swift when I try to do calculus. I usually fail.
